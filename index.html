Kayleigh Skinner

Journalist based in Jackson, Miss.

About

Kayleigh Skinner currently reports on politics, education, state government and how they intersect for Mississippi Today, a nonprofit digital news organization. She has also written about education inequality in Mississippi at The Hechinger Report and covered Memphis and Shelby County while at Chalkbeat Tennessee and The Commercial Appeal. She graduated from the University of Mississippi in 2014.
