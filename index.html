ABOUT

I'm a Canadian journalist working in Mississippi. I report on politics, education, state government and how they intersect for Mississippi Today, a nonprofit digital news organization. I am passionate about covering issues of race and education and using data to shed light on these topics.

I've also written about education inequality in the Mississippi as an intern at The Hechinger Report, and covered Memphis and Shelby County schools while at Chalkbeat Tennessee. Before returning to Jackson to work at Mississippi Today, I worked as a general assigment reporter at The Memphis Commercial Appeal. I graduated from the University of Mississippi in 2014.
